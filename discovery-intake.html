Discovery call script.

Your calm, confident guide for a first call. Ask, listen, take notes in the margins. The goal: understand their problem and decide if you're a fit — not to pitch.

Warm up

1

Tell me a bit about your business and what a typical week looks like for you right now.

2

What made you start looking for help at this particular moment?

Listen for the trigger — that's the real pain.

Dig into the problem

3

Which tasks are eating the most time or causing the most stress?

4

What have you tried before, and what didn't work about it?

5

If we cleared those off your plate, what would you do with the time?

Their answer = the value you're really selling.

Logistics & fit

6

Roughly how many hours a week do you think this needs?

7

Which tools do you use day to day? (email, calendar, project tool, etc.)

8

How do you prefer to communicate, and how often?

9

Do you have a budget range in mind for this?

Close

10

What questions do you have for me?

11

Here's what I'd suggest as next steps… {tee up the proposal}. Sound good?
