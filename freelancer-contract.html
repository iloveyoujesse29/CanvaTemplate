1Services

2Term & schedule

This agreement begins on the start date above and continues {month-to-month / until {end date}}. Provider will generally work {days/hours, e.g. Mon–Fri, up to X hours/week}, excluding holidays unless agreed otherwise.

3Fees & payment

Client agrees to pay {$X per month / $X per hour}, invoiced {monthly in advance / bi-weekly}. Payment is due within {14} days of each invoice. Late payments may pause work and incur a {X%} late fee. All fees are non-refundable once work has begun for that period.

4Revisions & approvals

Deliverables include up to {X} rounds of reasonable revisions. Additional revisions are billed at {$X/hour}. Client will provide feedback and approvals within {X} business days so work can stay on schedule.

5Cancellation & termination

Either party may end this agreement with {14} days' written notice. Client remains responsible for fees for work completed and for the current billing period. Provider may pause or end work immediately for non-payment or breach of this agreement.

6Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep each other's private business information confidential and to use it only to perform this agreement. This obligation continues after the agreement ends.

7Independent contractor

Provider is an independent contractor, not an employee. Provider is responsible for their own taxes, equipment, and business expenses, and may work with other clients.

8Ownership

On full payment, finished deliverables become Client's property. Provider may keep copies and non-confidential samples for their portfolio unless agreed otherwise in writing.

9Limitation of liability

Provider will perform services with reasonable care and skill. To the extent allowed by law, Provider's total liability under this agreement is limited to the fees paid for the affected work. Provider is not liable for indirect or consequential losses.

10Entire agreement

This document is the entire agreement between the parties and replaces any prior discussions. Changes must be in writing and signed by both parties. If any part is found unenforceable, the rest remains in effect.
